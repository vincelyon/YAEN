About The Program

Digital Skills & Software Training

YAEN's Digital Skills program empowers youth with essential technology skills for the modern workforce. Our comprehensive training covers graphic design, web development, coding, data analysis, and Microsoft Office applications.

Delivered both online and in-person, our programs are designed to be accessible to youth from all backgrounds. We've trained over 300 graduates who have gone on to secure employment in tech companies or start their own digital businesses.
