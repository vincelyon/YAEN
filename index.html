About Us

Welcome to YAEN

The Youth Academic Empowerment Network (YAEN) is a legally registered youth-led nonprofit organization under the National Youth Development Council Act (2024) in Zambia. We champion education, innovation, mentorship, and economic empowerment for underserved youth.

Through inclusive programs, strategic partnerships, and community-led solutions, YAEN empowers the next generation of leaders with tools to thrive in academics, entrepreneurship, digital technology, and civic leadership.
